About HarborNest

HarborNest is a premium, single-boat charter based on the Côte d’Azur. We focus on clean and safe trips with high-speed internet, great coffee and breakfast, music, and professional photos—so your time on the water feels effortless and memorable.

What we believe

  • Hospitality first — thoughtful details from booking to disembarkation
  • Reliability — well-maintained boat, professional crew, and safety briefings
  • Flexibility — from business mornings to sunset cruises and day escapes

The boat

We operate a Lagoon 40 catamaran with generous outdoor lounge spaces, a bright salon, foredeck trampolines, and comfortable cabins. Amenities include Wi-Fi, Bluetooth audio, coffee & breakfast (morning), and water toys.

Your safety is our priority: full safety gear, children lifejackets on request, first-aid kit, and clear briefings. We maintain appropriate insurance coverage for the charter activity.
